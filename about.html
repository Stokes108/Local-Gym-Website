Washtenaw Gym

Welcome to Washtenaw Gym - the ultimate fitness destination! Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with top-of-the-line cardio machines, weightlifting equipment, and a refreshing swimming pool for an all-around workout experience. Take a dip in our pool, run on the treadmill, and join one of our invigorating yoga classes. Our expert trainers are always on hand to guide and motivate you to achieve your fitness goals. Come and join the Washtenaw Gym community today and transform your mind and body.
